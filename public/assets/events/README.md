## Image Specifications

- **Dimensions**: 600px Ã— 400px (or similar 3:2 ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 500KB each
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, professional
- **Content**: Should show:
  - People engaged in activities
  - Happy, diverse participants
  - Clear focus (not blurry)
  - Authentic moments from actual events
  - Proper permissions for recognizable faces

## Tips for Good Event Photos

1. **Action Shots**: Capture people actively participating
2. **Group Photos**: Show the scale and community aspect
3. **Diverse Representation**: Include people of different ages and backgrounds
4. **Natural Lighting**: Well-lit photos without harsh shadows
5. **Context**: Include some venue/setting details
6. **High Quality**: Sharp, in-focus images
7. **Emotional Connection**: Show joy, engagement, and learning

## Fallback

If images are not available, placeholder images will be shown automatically with relevant colors and text.
