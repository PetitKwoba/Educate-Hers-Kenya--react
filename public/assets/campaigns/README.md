# Campaign Images

This folder contains images for donation campaigns displayed on the Donate page.

## Required Images

Add the following campaign images:

- [ ] `school-supplies.jpg` - School supplies for girls
- [ ] `mentorship.jpg` - Mentorship program activities
- [ ] `stem.jpg` - STEM workshops and activities
- [ ] `scholarship.jpg` - Scholarship recipients or students
- [ ] `digital.jpg` - Digital learning devices (tablets/laptops)
- [ ] `outreach.jpg` - Community outreach activities

## Image Specifications

- **Dimensions**: 400px Ã— 250px (or similar 16:10 ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File Size**: Under 300KB each
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit, professional looking
- **Content**: Should show:
  - Girls engaged in learning activities
  - Community impact
  - Educational materials
  - Authentic moments (not stock photos if possible)

## Tips

1. Use real photos from your organization's activities when possible
2. Ensure faces are visible and subjects look happy/engaged
3. Good lighting and clear focus
4. Avoid overly posed or artificial-looking shots
5. Represent diversity and inclusion
6. Get proper permissions for photos with identifiable people

## Fallback

If images are not available, placeholder images will be shown automatically.
